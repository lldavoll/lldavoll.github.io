### SPAN 102: Second Semester Spanish
**Institution:** University of Arizona  
**Terms:** Spring 2025 / Fall 2025 / Spring 2026

Spanish 102 is designed as an introduction to Spanish as an additional language and the many facets of Hispanic culture. This course begins to develop all four core language skills—speaking, listening, reading, and writing.
_For more information about the course check the following [link](https://catalog.arizona.edu/courses/0317091)_

### SPAN 101: Introductory Spanish
**Institution:** University of Arizona  
**Term:** Fall 2024  

Spanish 101 is designed as an introduction to Spanish as an additional language and the many facets of Hispanic culture. This course begins to develop all four core language skills—speaking, listening, reading, and writing. The course highly recommends classroom participation and requires approximately two hours of preparation outside of class for every hour of class instruction.

_For more information about the course check the following [link](https://catalog.arizona.edu/courses/0317051)_
